Clash of Clan is a 2012 free to play mobile strategy video game developed and published by finnish game developer Supercell.The game was released for ios paltforms on Agust 2,2012and on Google play Android on October 7,2013.The game is set in a fantasy themed prersistent world where the player is a chief of a village. Clash of Clans tasks player to build their own village using the resources gained from attacking the other player's village with troops;earning rewards, buying them with medals or by producing them at their own village.The main resources are gold,elixer and dark elixer.Players can join others clan or can create their own clan. Player can take and donate troops as they like and they can participate in Clan gaames and clan wars.
